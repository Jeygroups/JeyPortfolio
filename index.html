JEYANTH AYYAPARAJ

Founder & CEO of JEY Groups and Swivel IT & Training Institute, and Trustee of Dolphin Education and Rural Development Trust, Theni.

Born in Bodinayakanur, Tamil Nadu on September 10, 2000, Jeyanth Ayyaparaj is a rising Indian entrepreneur and educator committed to digital empowerment and rural upliftment.

Early Life & Education

Jeyanth earned his BE in Mechanical Engineering and pursued a Mainframe Development course through Udemy. His passion led him to join IBM, where he worked for 4 years as a Mainframe developer before beginning his entrepreneurial journey.

Career & Startup

He founded Swivel IT & Training Institute to provide hands-on IT training with real-time technologies such as Full Stack, Mainframe, and Cloud. His institute has trained and mentored over 100+ students from rural backgrounds.

Specializations: Mentorships, Mainframe, Career Guidance
Mentorships: Over 100 students trained

Founded in 2022, Dolphin Trust works under the JEY Groups and supports students in rural Tamil Nadu by offering scholarships, school development, and awareness programs.
